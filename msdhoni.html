MS Dhoni

Achievements and Legacy

MS Dhoni debuted for India in 2004 and quickly rose to prominence with his aggressive batting
and sharp wicketkeeping. In 2007, he was appointed captain of the Indian cricket team. Under his
leadership, the team achieved the No.1 ranking in Test cricket and won major ICC tournaments.
Dhoni is famed for his ability to stay cool under pressure and for making bold decisions onthe field.
